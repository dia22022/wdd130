The beauty of wildlife photos lies in their ability to captivate our imagination and connect us with the natural world. These images evoke a sense of wonder, appreciation, and awareness of the diverse and intricate ecosystems that exist in our planet. It reveals the stunning and often hidden aspects of animals, plants, and ecosystems. They provide a window into the wild, enabling people to see animals and environments they might never encounter in person. Wildlife photographs use light composition and timing to create visually appealing images. This includes rare behaviors, interactions between species, or even the challenges and triumphs of survival. The spirit of adventure adds an element of excitement to the art of wildlife photography.

Identity photography is a term that can be encompassed as a broad range of photography styles and subjects related to personal identity, self-expression, and individual or group representation. Photography allows individuals to express themselves in their own terms. It is a platform for people to showcase their personal style and aspects of their identity that are important to them. They can reveal the life experiences, struggles, and accomplishments of themselves or groups, helping viewers to empathize and connect with the subjects on a deeper level.
